Maria is an “old school” developer who worked her way to a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science by using what she was learning in school to create websites and develop software. The websites were created with HTML written in Notepad and were heavy on the graphics, which were created in Photoshop CS and Flash. She was passionate about developing, but living in Wichita, Kansas, known as the Air Capital of the World for all of the aircraft manufacturers in the area, it was inevitable that she would end up in the aviation industry. When an opportunity arose to work with the Federal Aviation Administration and be the first non-manufacturer company to issue Temporary RVSM Approvals, she took it. This is what began Premium RVSM Services, LLC. Cessna kept her busy with their international business jet clients until two overseas manufacturers began producing business jets. Between the new competition and the recession/mortgage crisis, Cessna saw a sharp decrease in sales and was forced to lay off over half of their workforce. Premium RVSM Services was hit hard and this took Maria on a journey that has finally circled back around to developing. She recently updated her skills through Bottega Tech’s Full Stack Web Engineer courses and worked as an apprentice student mentor/support tech for Bottega. She is now the NW Kansas Tech Lead for Rural and Remote.

She isn’t very good at proofreading her own work and her “can-do” attitude can be a little over the top at times, but her organizational and trouble-shooting skills will make her a valuable asset. She brings a lot to the table with her extensive experience with remote work and dedication to always do her best, as well as her strong work ethic.
