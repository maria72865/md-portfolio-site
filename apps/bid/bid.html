Screen 3 of 3 offered a dropdown populated with 38 various aircraft models, input for expected wind direction and speed (it's a pilot thing!), displayed total fuel required based on the aircraft model and mileage, input for current fuel cost which triggered the fuel cost calculation, and inputs for crew costs, misc costs and insurance cost.
